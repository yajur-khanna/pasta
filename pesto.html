Pesto Pasta

A quick vegetarian dish where spaghetti is tossed with homemade basil pesto and a splash of pasta water for a creamy sauce. Adapted from Love & Lemons【748169355998302†L156-L183】.

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe homemade basil pesto (prepared with ⅓ cup olive oil, or use ⅓ cup store‑bought pesto to start)
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Prepare the pesto according to your favorite recipe. If using store‑bought pesto, start with about ⅓ cup and add more to taste.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water, then drain.
  3. Transfer the pasta to a large skillet. Add the pesto and ½ cup of the reserved pasta water and toss to coat, adding more water as needed to achieve a lightly creamy sauce.
  4. Season to taste with salt, pepper or a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve topped with grated Parmesan and fresh basil.
