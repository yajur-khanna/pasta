Fettuccine Alfredo

This rich and creamy dish uses butter, heavy cream and Parmigiano Reggiano to coat wide fettuccine noodles. Recipe adapted from Once Upon a Chef【99862735203513†L206-L218】【99862735203513†L221-L234】.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fettuccine
  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • A pinch of freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook until al dente according to the package directions. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. In the same pot over medium‑low heat, melt the butter. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant but not browned, about 1–2 minutes. Stir in the heavy cream, salt, pepper and nutmeg and bring to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for about two minutes to thicken slightly.
  3. Gradually stir in the grated cheese until melted.
  4. Add the drained fettuccine back to the pot and toss with the sauce until well coated and warmed through. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time to achieve the desired sauce consistency. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve immediately.
